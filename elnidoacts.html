Activities

    El Nido Island Hopping Party Boat

    Enjoy vibrant beach parties where you can dance to live music, sip cocktails, and mingle with fellow travelers, all while enjoying the breathtaking views of El Nido’s stunning beaches.

    Kayaking

    Paddle through crystal-clear waters and explore hidden lagoons. Kayaking is a peaceful yet exciting way to discover El Nido's pristine coastline and secluded beaches.

    Lounge at Nacpan Beach

    Experience the beauty of Nacpan Beach, one of El Nido’s most stunning stretches of coastline. With its powdery white sand and crystal-clear turquoise waters, this beach is the perfect place to unwind and soak in the sun.

    Take a Dip in Bulalacao Waterfalls

    Escape the heat and immerse yourself in the refreshing waters of Bulalacao Waterfalls, a hidden gem nestled in the heart of El Nido. With its crystal-clear waters cascading down the lush green cliffs, this serene spot offers the perfect opportunity to swim, relax, and connect with nature.

    Scuba Diving

    Dive into the underwater world of El Nido and discover its colorful coral reefs and abundant marine life. Whether you’re a beginner or experienced diver, the waters here are mesmerizing.

    Ride the Las Cabanas Beach Zipline

    Experience an adrenaline rush as you soar above the crystal-clear waters of Las Cabanas Beach on the thrilling zipline ride. Feel the breeze on your face as you glide through the air, taking in breathtaking views of the coastline and lush greenery.

    Do the El Nido Via Ferrata Canopy Walk

    Hike through the lush landscapes of El Nido and enjoy panoramic views of the coastline. Trekking to hidden viewpoints is a great way to experience the natural beauty of this paradise.

    Visit Duli Beach – Pristine White Sand Beach

    Head to Duli Beach for a more secluded and peaceful experience. Known for its soft sands and clear waters, it’s perfect for swimming, surfing, or just enjoying the calm environment.

    Watch the Sunset at Las Cabanas

    One of the most stunning experiences in El Nido is watching the sunset at Las Cabanas Beach. As the sun sets, the sky is filled with vibrant shades of orange and purple.
