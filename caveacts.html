Activities

    Explore the Caves

    Embark on a journey into the heart of Tabon Caves, where history comes alive in the intricate rock formations and ancient chambers. Marvel at the fascinating stalactites and stalagmites that have taken millennia to form, and imagine what life was like for the early settlers who called these caves home.

    See the Tabon Man

    Get a glimpse of humanity's origins by visiting the site where the Tabon Man was discovered. This archaeological treasure is a testament to the Philippines' prehistoric heritage, providing valuable insights into the lives of early humans who thrived thousands of years ago. A must-see for history enthusiasts.

    Picnic on the Beach

    Unwind with a picnic at one of the serene beaches surrounding the caves. With stunning views of the horizon, soft sands underfoot, and the gentle sound of waves lapping at the shore, it's the perfect way to relax and connect with nature after exploring the caves.

    Swim and Snorkel

    Dive into the clear blue waters around Tabon and explore the vibrant underwater world. Swim alongside colorful fish and coral reefs, or simply enjoy the refreshing sea breeze as you soak up the sun. Perfect for adventurers and water lovers alike.

    Visit Sidanao Island

    Escape to the tranquil Sidanao Island, just a short trip from Tabon Caves. This hidden gem boasts picturesque landscapes, lush greenery, and a peaceful ambiance that will rejuvenate your soul. It's an ideal spot for those seeking solitude and natural beauty.
