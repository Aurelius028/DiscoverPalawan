Activities

    Swim in Kayangan Lake

    Experience the crystal-clear waters of Kayangan Lake, often regarded as the cleanest lake in the Philippines. Surrounded by towering limestone cliffs and lush greenery, this breathtaking destination is perfect for a refreshing swim.

    Take a Dip in Barracuda Lake

    Dive into the mystical Barracuda Lake, known for its striking underwater rock formations and unique thermocline layers. It’s a paradise for adventurous divers seeking a one-of-a-kind experience.

    Explore Siete Pecados

    Snorkel in the vibrant marine sanctuary of Siete Pecados, where colorful corals and diverse marine life thrive. This spot is perfect for underwater photography and nature lovers.

    Snorkel and Dive in Malcapuya Island

    Discover the pristine beauty of Malcapuya Island, boasting powdery white sands and turquoise waters. It’s an idyllic getaway for snorkeling, diving, and soaking up the sun.

    Discover the Beauty of Twin Lagoon

    Navigate through the enchanting Twin Lagoon, where dramatic limestone cliffs surround turquoise waters. Swim or kayak between the lagoons for a magical experience.

    Relax in Maquinit Hot Spring

    Unwind in the soothing Maquinit Hot Spring, a natural saltwater spring surrounded by mangroves. It’s the perfect way to relax after a day of adventures.

    Travel to Culion Island for a Historical Day Tour

    Explore Culion Island, rich in history and culture. Visit the Culion Museum and Church to learn about its past as a leper colony and admire the stunning views from the fortress ruins.

    Get a Panoramic View of Coron in Mt. Tapyas

    Hike up Mt. Tapyas for a breathtaking panoramic view of Coron town and its surrounding islands. The climb is rewarded with a stunning sunset.

    Try Overnight Camping in Busuanga

    Camp under the stars on Busuanga Island, surrounded by serene beaches and lush forests. It’s an unforgettable adventure for nature lovers.

    Visit Calauit Safari Park

    Step into an African-inspired wildlife sanctuary at Calauit Safari Park, home to giraffes, zebras, and other exotic animals. Perfect for a family-friendly day trip.
